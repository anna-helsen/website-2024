FLOATING (2022)

Video Projection
Moving Image Collage



A video projection on the main square in Karlsruhe, collaging narratives of societal norms and the fluidity of life.

An experimental video projection, collaging footage to create a narrative that poses questions about femininity, hydromedia and hydrofeminism and the fluidity between different norms and social constructs. The project was realised using After Effects.

This project took place at the central square in Karlsruhe, where the video was projected onto the Weinbrenner Church. This project was created in the seminar “Go Public” at HfG (University of Art and Design Karlsruhe).
